Featured Artist

Elena Vasquez

b. 1978, Buenos Aires · Based in New York

Elena Vasquez works at the intersection of gesture and architecture, creating monumental paintings that negotiate between the controlled and the accidental.

Her practice draws from diverse influences — from action painting to minimalist sculpture — yet resists easy categorisation. Each canvas becomes a landscape of compressed decisions, where colour and mark create layered environments that reward close looking.

Vasquez has exhibited extensively across Europe, North and South America, and Asia. Her work is held in major collections including the MoMA, Tate Modern, and the Guggenheim Bilbao.
