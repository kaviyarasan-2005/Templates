Our Story

About Artora

Eighty-five years of dedication to the transformative power of art.

Mission

Art that moves the world forward

Artora Gallery exists to connect people with art that challenges, inspires and transforms. Since 1941, we have been a vital cultural institution in the heart of the city.

We believe that art is not a luxury but a necessity — a lens through which we understand ourselves and each other. Our programming is guided by a commitment to diversity, excellence and access.
