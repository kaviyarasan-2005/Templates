About the Exhibition

An exploration of liminal space and abstraction

Three internationally celebrated artists converge around a shared inquiry: what exists between certainty and doubt, between presence and absence?

The Space Between Silence brings together the distinct practices of Elena Vasquez, Marcus Chen and Yuki Tanaka to create an immersive dialogue across media — painting, photography and sculpture — united by a fascination with the intangible.

Vasquez's monumental canvases pulse with compressed energy; Chen's photographic works capture the world's unguarded moments; Tanaka's sculptures occupy and transform the air around them. Together, they create a conversation that exceeds the sum of its parts.

The silence between notes makes the music. We are interested in those intervals — the charged spaces where meaning accumulates.

— James Okafor, Senior Curator
