Our Story

The Journey

From a small boutique studio to a global virtual platform.

2010

The Beginning

Intérieur was founded as a boutique interior design studio in SoHo, New York, focusing on luxury residential projects for local clients.

2015

Digital Transition

We pioneered the use of photorealistic 3D visualization to help clients truly see a space before we knocked down a single wall.

2020

Going Virtual

In response to a changing world, we launched our fully virtual design service, allowing us to serve clients across the globe without leaving our studio.

Present Day

Enterprise Expansion

Today, we offer both bespoke residential design and robust B2B virtual design infrastructure for large-scale real estate developers.
