How It Works

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with a 45-minute video call to discuss your vision, review photos of your current space, and establish your budget and functional needs.

2. Concept Development

Within 5 days, your designer will present two initial mood boards. You'll provide feedback so we can refine the design direction.

3. 3D Visuals & Floorplan

We'll map out the layout using your room measurements and provide 3D renders so you can see exactly how the new pieces fit together.

4. Final Shopping List

You receive a click-to-buy shopping list with access to our exclusive trade discounts. We can also handle the ordering process if desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are revisions included?

Yes, the Room Refresh service includes up to two rounds of revisions after the initial concept presentation.

Can you use my existing furniture?

Absolutely. Just provide photos and dimensions of the pieces you want to keep, and we'll integrate them seamlessly into the new design.
