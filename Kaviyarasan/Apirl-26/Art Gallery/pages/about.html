Est. 1998

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by art collector and visionary Isabella Moretti, Aurelia Gallery began as a small exhibition space in downtown Manhattan with a bold mission: to bridge the gap between established masters and emerging voices in contemporary art.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown into one of the city's most respected galleries, hosting over 300 exhibitions, representing 150+ artists, and building lasting relationships with collectors, institutions, and art lovers worldwide.

Our commitment remains unchanged: to curate meaningful experiences that connect people with art that challenges, inspires, and transforms.

Our Journey

Gallery Timeline

1998

The Beginning

Isabella Moretti opens the first Aurelia Gallery space on Gallery Row with an inaugural exhibition of 12 emerging artists.

2005

International Recognition

Aurelia Gallery receives the International Contemporary Art Award, establishing its reputation on the global stage.

2012

Expansion

Gallery expands to a 10,000 sq ft space, adding restoration facilities and a sculpture garden.

2020

Digital Innovation

Launch of virtual exhibition platform, bringing art to global audiences with immersive digital experiences.

2026

Today & Beyond

Hosting 12+ exhibitions annually, representing 150+ artists, and continuing to shape the future of contemporary art.
