Our Methodology

The Authentication Process

1

Initial Assessment

Preliminary condition review, preliminary edition identification, and risk assessment by a senior bibliographer.

2

Forensic Analysis

Paper stock dating, ink composition testing, typeface matching against period catalogues, and binding technique analysis.

3

Provenance Investigation

Library bookplates, ownership inscriptions, auction records, and dealer stamps traced through our proprietary archival network.

4

Certification Issued

A numbered, signed Certificate of Authenticity issued with full bibliographic detail and our liability guarantee.
