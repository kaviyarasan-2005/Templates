Our Heritage

Preserving The Physical Architecture of Knowledge

Founded in 1894, the Antiquarian Rare Bookstore has been a haven for scholars, collectors, and institutions worldwide. We believe a rare book is more than words on paper; it is an artifact of human history, complete with the marginalia, bindings, and provenance of generations past.

Our resident curators are foremost authorities in appraisal, bookbinding restoration, and literary forensics, dedicating their lives to the stewardship of these delicate volumes.
