Precision Machinery & Technology

CNC Machining Centers

60+ 5-axis machining centers including Mazak VARIAXIS i-500, Okuma MULTUS B400, and DMG Mori NHX 5000. Maximum workpiece: 1200mm × 800mm × 650mm.

Metrology Equipment

ZEISS CONTURA CMM with scanning probe, Renishaw Equator gauging systems, portable Faro arm, and Keyence digital microscopes in climate-controlled inspection room.

Laser & EDM Systems

Trumpf TruLaser 5030 10kW fiber laser, Sodick wire EDM systems, and LPT die-sinking EDM for complex cavities and intricate features.

Casting & Forging

50-ton horizontal die casting cell, vertical investment casting furnaces, and 2,500-ton hydraulic press for forging billets up to 300mm diameter.

Surface Treatment Plant

40-tank anodizing line (Type II & III), hard chrome plating, electroless nickel, PVD coating system, and automated powder coating booth.

Robotic Automation

12 FANUC robotic welding cells, automated part loading/unloading on 20 machining centers, and AGV material transport system throughout the facility.
