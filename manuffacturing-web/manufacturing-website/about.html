Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Engineering Excellence

ForgeCoreManufacturing was founded in 1994 by Thomas Reeves, a veteran aerospace engineer who believed that American precision manufacturing could compete and win on a global stage.

Starting with two CNC machines in a 10,000 sq ft Detroit facility, we have grown into a 200,000 sq ft manufacturing campus with over 450 employees, serving clients in 40+ countries.

Today, we are a tier-1 supplier to major aerospace and automotive OEMs, holding certifications in AS9100D, IATF 16949, and ISO 9001:2015.

Zero Defect Culture — Quality is not inspected, it's built in at every stage.

Client Partnership — We act as an extension of your engineering team.

Continuous Improvement — Kaizen drives our operations every day.

Sustainability — ISO 14001 certified with carbon reduction targets.
