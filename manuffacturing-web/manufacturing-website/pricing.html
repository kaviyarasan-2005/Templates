Pricing FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

Are there setup or tooling fees?

For standard CNC machining, no tooling fees apply. Custom tooling (fixtures, special form tools) is quoted separately when required. Die casting and forging dies are separately quoted as capital tooling.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, subscription plans can be cancelled with 30 days written notice. Active production orders will be completed at the contracted pricing. Part-month billing applies for cancellations mid-cycle.

Do prices include shipping?

Part pricing is FOB Detroit facility. Shipping charges are added at checkout based on destination, weight, and service level. Domestic ground shipping is typically $25–$150 for most orders.

What payment terms are available?

Starter: net 15 or credit card. Professional: net 30 upon credit approval. Enterprise: net 45 or custom terms. We accept ACH, wire transfer, and major credit cards. Subscription fees are auto-billed monthly or annually.
