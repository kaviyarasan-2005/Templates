What We Offer

End-to-End Manufacturing Solutions

Six core service areas supported by a team of 200+ engineers and technicians.

CNC Precision Machining

5-axis simultaneous machining on Mazak, Okuma, and DMG Mori centers. Tolerances to ±0.001mm. Materials: aluminum, titanium, Inconel, steel, copper, and engineered plastics.

Get Quote

Metal Fabrication

Full-service sheet metal: laser cutting (10kW fiber, up to 40mm), CNC press brake forming, robotic welding (TIG/MIG), and sub-assembly services for structures and enclosures.

Get Quote

Quality Assurance

ZEISS CMM inspection, SPC analysis, first article inspection (FAI) per AS9102, incoming material inspection, and full material traceability. NIST-traceable calibration.

Get Quote

Rapid Prototyping

48-hour prototype turnaround. CNC machined prototypes from solid billet. Any material, any complexity. Full engineering support and DFM analysis included at no extra charge.

Get Quote

Surface Treatment

In-house type II and type III anodizing, hard chrome, electroless nickel, PVD coating (TiN, TiAlN), powder coating in any RAL/Pantone color, and passivation of stainless steel.

Get Quote

Supply Chain Management

Vendor-managed inventory (VMI), kanban replenishment programs, blanket orders, and complete supply chain visibility via our customer portal with real-time order tracking.
